Welcome to the Teacher Workspace!

A comprehensive guide to managing your IHBB classroom tools.

My Classes & Roster Management

Create classrooms and generate unique join codes for your students. Monitor your roster and see who has joined your class at any time.

AI-Powered Draft Builder

Use the Draft Builder to generate targeted quiz questions based on specific topics, regions, or eras. Perfect for creating unique study material in seconds.

Custom Assignment Builder

Beyond AI generation, you can hand-pick questions from our library or use filters to build the perfect quiz for your students' needs.

Live Bee Sessions

Host real-time, interactive quiz sessions to engage your entire class. Students join via your room code for a high-energy trivia experience.

Advanced Class Analytics

Track average scores, completion rates, and practice frequency. Identify knowledge gaps across your entire class to focus your instruction.

Workspace Preferences

In Account Settings, you can set default classes, builder modes, and question counts to streamline your daily workflow.

DeepSeek Assistant & Hub

Access the Practice Hub to use the DeepSeek sidebar for AI-powered research, study aids, and session planning.
