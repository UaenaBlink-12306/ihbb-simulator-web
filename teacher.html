Updated the student My Classes join-code field so browsers and password managers are less likely to autofill unrelated saved information.

Account Settings Overhaul

April 21, 2026

The Account page has been redesigned so that saving profile basics (DisplayName, Email, Password) and workspace preferences are decoupled and immediately accessible without excessive scrolling. You can also now update your account password directly from this tab.

Feature Tooltips

April 21, 2026

Added descriptive hover tooltips to lesser-known features like DeepSeek, Coach modes, and Blind Spot Analytics so you can easily understand what they do before toggling them.

App Walkthroughs

April 21, 2026

Added an in-app walkthrough for new accounts to explain features like Classes & Assignments, Assignment Builder, Live Bee, and Class Analytics. You can revisit it anytime via the "Help / Tour" button on the dashboard.

AI Notebook Weak Spot Generation & What's New Page

April 21, 2026

Connected the AI Notebook directly to DeepSeek generation allowing you to automatically generate new practice questions targeting your weakest regions and eras. Added the "What's New" page to track future updates!
